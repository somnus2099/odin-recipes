Peanut Butter Jellly & Bacon Sandwich

Description

Do you have a boring old PB&J just layin' around? Add Bacon!

Ingredients

  • Peanut Butter
  • Jelly
  • 4 strips of bacon
  • two slices of bread

Steps

  1. fry 4 pieces of bacon to desired crispness.
  2. while the bacon is cooking, slather a healthy portion of jelly and peanut butter on two slices of bread.
  3. put bacon on the sandwich.
  4. eat the sandwich.
  5. tell all your friends about the sandwich.
  6. call a family member, just to check in.
  7. get that new haircut you've always wanted.
  8. put away peanut butter and jelly.
  9. tie up the bread bag.
