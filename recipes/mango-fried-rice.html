Mango Fried Rice

Description

This mango fried rice is a veritable flavor explosion. If you like that fruity funk in your giddyup, then hop aboard this rice train!

Next Stop: Flavortown!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 mango, cubed
  • 1/2 cup of leftover basmati rice
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup cashews
  • 1/2 bell pepper, sliced
  • 1/2 red onion, sliced
  • soy sauce
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 4 tbs olive oil

Directions

  1. heat dry pan to medium and toast cashews just until fragrant. Remove from pan.
  2. lower heat to medium-low. melt butter and scramble eggs. Set aside.
  3. raise heat back to medium. Add olive oil and sesame oil and sautee garlic, onions, and bell peppers until soft
  4. raise heat to medium high and add rice, allowing the smoke from the pan or wok to infused in the rice.
  5. stirring the rice often, add the scrambled egg back into the rice. add Mango
  6. finish with desired amount of soy sauce, then sprinkle on cashews.
  7. plate and serve
  8. Optionally, let that rice transcend with a bit of cilantro and extra soy sauce.
